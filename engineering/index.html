Duxbury Robotics Engineering Week

Open for all Alden students

The DHS Robotics Team will be holding a Robotics Activity Workshop for all Alden students with a passion for technology! Students will be given the opportunity to discover the excitement of engineering through activities in constructing and operating a functioning robot.

Wednesday, February, 25th to Friday, 27th from 2:30 to 4:30 pm.

The cost is $30
