Engineering

Students have the opportunity to learn about the engineering and design process, and how it can be applied to create new and exciting technologies. Using industrial materials, our student engineers can achieve great success in constructing a robust robot.

Programing

Our programming team expands their horizons as they learn to use logic to problem solve. Using advanced programming languages, students learn to push their own abilities beyond their self-perceived intellectual capacity.

Managment

Any organization or company requires an organizing force. Our management team is responsible for managing the team’s finances, and generating public interest in robotics. Along the way, students learn the necessary skills that are required in the business world.

Why is robotics important? Because of the doors it opens.

Robotics is the result of the marriage between mechanical and software engineering. Robotics offers its patrons unique opportunities to learn and develop. Duxbury Robotics is a catalyst for robotics and helps to nourish the youth’s ever growing interest in robotics.
